Frequently Asked Questions
How does it all work? 

You complete our 100-point profile questionnaire so we can get to know you and what you're looking for in a partner. Then the old matchmaker goes to work. Our algorithm will pair you with carefully vetted potential matches every day.  If you're both interested, you can start to get to know each other.

How many matches do I get in a day?

You get 3 matches every day with the free plan. If you upgrade to our premium offering, you will see 6 matches a day. 

How are my matches selected? 

The answers you provide to our profile questionnaire and our matchmaking algorithm pair you up with people who align your personality, lifestyle, interests, relationship values, and more! And our machine learning model gets smarter every time you match so we can refine your personal recommendations each day.
